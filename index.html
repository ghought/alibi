How to Play

Every day, a small crime has been committed. Five suspects each have an alibi — but one of them is lying.

Read the clues, cross-reference the alibis, and identify the liar.

  • Three clues are revealed at the start.
  • If you guess wrong, the suspect is cleared and a bonus clue is revealed.
  • Your score is the number of attempts it takes. Lower is better.

Scoring:

  • 1 attempt — Case Closed (perfect!)
  • 2 attempts — Sharp Eye
  • 3 attempts — Solid Work
  • 4 attempts — Dogged Detective
  • 5 attempts — Process of Elimination
